Big Five Personality Quiz

Openness to Experience

What it measures: Openness reflects your appetite for imagination, intellectual curiosity, creativity and variety. It captures how readily you seek out novel ideas, aesthetics and experiences.

If you score HIGH: you’re likely imaginative, original, intensely curious and comfortable with change and abstract ideas.
If you score LOW: you may prefer the familiar, value practicality over fantasy and feel uneasy with ambiguity or unconventional thinking.

Conscientiousness

What it measures: Conscientiousness gauges self-discipline, organisation, reliability and goal-directed behaviour. It shows how diligently you plan, follow rules and persist with tasks.

If you score HIGH: you’re organised, responsible, detail-oriented and driven to achieve long-term goals.
If you score LOW: you may be spontaneous, flexible and comfortable with disorder, but also more prone to procrastination and missed details.

Extraversion

What it measures: Extraversion captures social energy, assertiveness, enthusiasm and the tendency to seek external stimulation. It reflects how invigorated you feel by people and activity.

If you score HIGH: you’re outgoing, talkative, energetic and often take leadership roles.
If you score LOW: you’re reserved, self-contained and recharge with quiet time, finding large crowds draining.

Agreeableness

What it measures: Agreeableness reflects empathy, compassion, trust and the desire for social harmony. It shows how readily you consider and accommodate other people’s needs.

If you score HIGH: you’re cooperative, kind-hearted, trusting and quick to compromise.
If you score LOW: you’re frank, competitive and comfortable with conflict, preferring directness over diplomacy.

Neuroticism

What it measures: Neuroticism represents emotional volatility, stress reactivity and proneness to negative feelings such as anxiety, anger and sadness.

If you score HIGH: you may experience frequent worry, mood swings and heightened sensitivity to stress or criticism.
If you score LOW: you tend to stay calm, resilient and emotionally stable, even in challenging situations.
